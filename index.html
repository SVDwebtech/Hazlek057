About

Welcome to HAZLEK, your trusted electrical contracting company based in Modimolle and proudly serving the entire Limpopo Province. We specialize in special and hazardous installations across domestic, commercial, and industrial sectors.

With 18 years of experience in the construction industry, HAZLEK has a proven track record working on petrochemical and construction sites throughout South Africa and neighboring states. Our expertise lies in quality control and the issuance of Certificates of Compliance (CoC), ensuring that all our projects meet the highest standards of safety and excellence.

    Our Master Installation Electrician (MIE) provides expert services in specialized and hazardous electrical locations. With over 18 years of experience in the construction industry, we ensure all electrical installations are safe, compliant, and reliable.

  • Registered Electric Fence Installer (EFSI)

    As a registered Electric Fence Installer (EFSI), we offer comprehensive installation and maintenance services for electric fences, ensuring they meet all safety and compliance standards.

  • Site's Reticulation: Inspection, Installation, and Maintenance

    We manage and maintain site reticulation systems, providing thorough inspections, installations, and maintenance services to ensure efficient and safe electrical distribution.

  • Electrical Installations and Equipment Maintenance

    We handle the installation and maintenance of all electrical systems and equipment, including air conditioners, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.

  • Quality Control Services

    Our quality control services include inspecting new installations and verifying that all work meets relevant standards. We ensure all Certificates of Compliance (CoC) are correctly issued, providing peace of mind and regulatory compliance.

  • Fuel Storage Facilities

    We specialize in electrical work for fuel storage facilities, a critical area that requires a Master Installation Electrician. We handle installations and issue the necessary Certificates of Compliance as per SANS codes, ensuring safety and adherence to legal standards.

  • Statutory Inspections

    We assist with statutory inspections, legally required at least every two years, ensuring your electrical installations comply with all regulations and standards.

  • Solar Solutions

    We provide solar installation and maintenance services, offering sustainable and efficient energy solutions tailored to your needs.
